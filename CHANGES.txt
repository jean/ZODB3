3.11.0a1 (2012-11-19)
=====================

New Features
------------

- ZODB3 has been split into 4 separate packages:

  - persistent 4.0
  - BTrees 4.0
  - ZODB 4.0
  - ZEO 4.0

  The ZODB3 distribution is simply a meta-distribution, for backward
  compatibility, that depends the others.

See the changes logs in the other packages.
